Example 2.1.17. Estimation Using Rectangles.

Estimate the area between the \(x\)-axis and the graph of \(f(x)=x^2+1\) over the interval \([0,3]\) by using the three rectangles shown in Figure 2.1.18.
🔗
Solution.
The areas of the three rectangles are 1 unit\(^2\text{,}\) 2 unit\(^2\text{,}\) and 5 unit\(^2\text{.}\) Using these rectangles, our area estimate is 8 unit\(^2\text{.}\)
